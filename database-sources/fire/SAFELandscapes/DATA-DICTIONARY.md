## Notes

The S.A.F.E. Landscapes guidebook provides:
- Fire-safe landscape design principles for Southern California WUI
- Plant selection guidance (general categories rather than species-level data)
- Defensible space zone recommendations
- Irrigation and maintenance guidelines for fire safety

The plant data that does exist is largely covered by other datasets in this collection (UCForestProductsLab, OaklandFireSafe, BethkeUCCE2016).
