# Data Dictionary: UF_IFAS_FirewiseShrubs

**Source ID:** `FIRE-10`
**Description:** UF/IFAS Extension "Selecting Firewise Shrubs to Reduce Wildfire Risk" — companion fact sheet to the NIST/USDA experimental flammability study. No unique data beyond what's in NIST_USDA_Flammability.
**Status:** Source PDF archived. The 34 tested shrubs and their flammability rankings are already captured in `NIST_USDA_Flammability`.

## Notes

This 3-page fact sheet summarizes the same 34-shrub experimental study:
- 22 low flammability (appropriate for firewise landscaping)
- 8 moderate flammability (use cautiously)
- 4 high flammability (not recommended)

The data is identical to `NIST_USDA_Flammability/plants.csv`. This folder exists to preserve the source PDF and citation.

## Source

Hermansen-Baez, A., Crandall, R.M., Zipperer, W.C., Long, A.J., Behm, A.L., McKinstry, D., and Andreu, A. "Fire in the Wildland-Urban Interface: Selecting Firewise Shrubs to Reduce Wildfire Risk." UF/IFAS Extension FOR272.
