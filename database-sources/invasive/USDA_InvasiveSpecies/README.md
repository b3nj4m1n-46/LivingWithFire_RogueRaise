# USDA National Invasive Species Information Center - Terrestrial Plants

**Source:** USDA National Invasive Species Information Center
**URL:** https://www.invasivespeciesinfo.gov/terrestrial/plants
**Plants:** 30 key terrestrial invasive species

## About

This is the USDA's curated list of priority terrestrial invasive plant species in the United States. These are species with significant ecological and economic impacts that are actively managed through federal programs.
