# OSU Extension - Top 5 Drought-Tolerant Plants for Western Oregon

**Source:** Oregon State University Extension
**URL:** https://extension.oregonstate.edu/collection/top-5-plants-unirrigated-landscapes-western-oregon
**Authors:** Heather Stoven and Neil Bell (2024-2026)
**Plants:** 24 cultivars/species proven drought-tolerant in OSU unirrigated trials
**Region:** Western Oregon
**Methodology:** Field trials without summer irrigation since 2000

## About

OSU researchers evaluated hundreds of plant cultivars in unirrigated field conditions in Western Oregon's Mediterranean climate. This collection represents the **top 5 performers** in each of 5 plant groups — the most rigorously tested drought-tolerant plants for the Pacific Northwest.

## Plant Groups

| Group | Count | Species |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Ceanothus (California Lilac) | 5 | C. 'Victoria', C. gloriosus, C. 'Blue Jeans', C. x delilianus, C. cuneatus |

| Arctostaphylos (Manzanita) | 5 | A. uva-ursi, A. nummularia, A. hookeri, A. columbiana, A. 'Sentinel' |
| Grevillea (Spider Flower) | 4 | G. australis, G. juniperina, G. 'Poorinda Leane', G. victoriae |
| Groundcover Shrubs | 5 | Artemisia alba, Baccharis pilularis, Cotoneaster glaucophyllus, Pseudodictamnus hirsutus, Phlomis italica |
